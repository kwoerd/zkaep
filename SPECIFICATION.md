# ZK-AEP Specification

Version: 0.1 Draft

## Purpose

Define a standardized protocol for proving athletic eligibility using zero-knowledge proofs while minimizing disclosure of underlying information.

## Scope

This document defines the protocol, not the eligibility rules.

## Important Limitation

ZK-AEP can preserve privacy during eligibility verification. It cannot, by itself, determine whether eligibility rules are lawful, fair, ethical, or defensible.

For DAO Sports, ZK-AEP should be treated as a technical privacy standard, not as the source of eligibility authority.

A defensible eligibility system also requires:

- approved credential issuers;
- informed consent;
- data minimization;
- clear division rules;
- appeal and exception procedures;
- anti-discrimination review;
- governance accountability;
- legal and medical/privacy review where applicable.
